Three Model Blind A/B Judged Test of Meta-Llama-3-8B-Instruct

(With and Without CREATE Framework)

I wanted to try a second test with a micro open-source LLM after the amazing results with Neomotron-Nano-12b-v2. I selected Meta-Llama-8B-Instruct from available models on Hugginchat, and a question which has bothered me since a child - how to help people understand the epistomological value of science, not as a 'Truth' claim, but as an experimental methodology which converges towards truth by iteration. Like the Nemotron test, I ran this without the pattern matching header and the uncertainty packet to keep weight down.

Blind A/B grading was performed by GPT-5.2, Claude Sonnet 4.5, and Grok 4. All scored heavily in favor of the CREATE enhanced version. Results were summarized by Gemini 3.0 Flash. Grok remained the highest over-estimator of model size (over-estimating both, but preserving a sizable gap in capacity), but even Claude, the most conservative grader, estimated a significantly larger parameter count for the CREATE enhanced Llama instance(13-70B vs 7-13B for the unenhanced version).
